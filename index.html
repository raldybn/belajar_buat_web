About Us

History

PT. Jafarel Mediatics (“Jafarel”) was founded on 27 January 2005, as per Notary deed Rini Soemintapoera, Jakarta Selatan no. 5/2005. Jafarel`s inception was based on the founders` social awareness and concern toward the inadequate medical services to the society.

Quality Work Process

In order to provide the expected service, Jafarel will educate and give extensive understanding of the importance of one`s health to the whole human resources. This expertise regarding health will start from, selective product selection, thorough control regarding stock material; sensitivity toward quality and hygiene medical paraphernalia, also knowledge oriented marketing strategy.
